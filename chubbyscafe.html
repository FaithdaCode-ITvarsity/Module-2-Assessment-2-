Chubby's Cafe

Established in 2010 in Pleasant Grove, it has quickly become a Utah County staple. Here are some key points about Chubby’s Cafe: Family-Owned and Operated: Chubby’s Cafe is a family-owned and operated restaurant. Home-Grown Flavors: Known for its home-grown flavors, the restaurant offers a wide variety of American dining favorites with a slight southern twist. Menu Highlights: Burgers: Chubby’s Cafe serves delicious burgers, including the popular Chubby Cheesesteak and the guacamole bacon burger. Other Options: If you’re not in the mood for burgers, you can choose from items like shrimp po-boys, codfish baskets, wings, and salads. Shakes and Floats: Pair your meal with shakes and floats, featuring flavors like cookie dough, peach cobbler, and classic Oreo. Kid’s Menu: There’s also a kid’s menu with options like cheeseburgers, mac 'n cheese, BBQ pork sandwiches, chicken tenders, and grilled cheese sandwiches. Chubby’s Cafe has expanded to multiple locations across Utah, including two upcoming locations in St. George.
